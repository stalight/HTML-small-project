Project 2 - Flip a Coin!

Choose a side for your coin, this is the side you will be betting on
for winning. You are able to change your side each round, the computer will
choose the latter. The first to reach a score of 10 wins the game!
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The restart button is there if you ever want to restart the game
or replay the game after the game is done!
